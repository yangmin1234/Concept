Jacque's Words

Feel free to read.

Jacque's words is a simple project.
There is GitHub repository link provided at the bottom of the page.

Jacque's words is powered by the Python framework Django. It's a simple blog where a person can log in, make a post and also view other posts and even comment on them.

Besides the blog, there are other features such as the polls application which is featured on the right side of the homepage. This feature allows a person to vote on a specific question which can only be provided by an admin. These questions are specifically designed to be interesting and perhaps thought-provoking.

It is recommended that the posts be unique, in the sense that they provide original content, a creative view or opinion on a certain subject or material. For more information feel free to visit How to make a post for instructions on posting.

Thank you for using this website, we hope you enjoy the content provided by some of our users and/or staff.

For more information, feel free to visit the contact page.
